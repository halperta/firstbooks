This website was designed to accompany the Reading the First Books Symposium, held May 30, 2017, at the University of Texas at Austin. Each link represents a book in the Primeros Libros collection. Follow the links to see rough transcriptions of sample pages produced using the Ocular Optical Character Recognition system. The transcriptions have not been reviewed or undergone post-processing of any kind, and were produced for experimental purposes using very limited amounts of data.

For more information about the project or the data, contact Hannah Alpert-Abrams (halperta@gmail.com).
